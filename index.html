How it works

Simple. Automatic. Effective.

Three steps. Fully set up for you. Running within 48 hours.

1

Lead comes in

A potential patient messages your WhatsApp, fills in a form on your website, or enquires via Instagram. Their details are captured automatically.

2

Within seconds, your AI receptionist sends a warm, personalised message. It answers questions, handles objections, and moves the conversation toward a booking.

The AI sends your booking link at the right moment. The patient picks a time. You see the appointment appear. No chasing, no missed enquiries.
